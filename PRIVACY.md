# Privacy Policy

Kumo is an Open Source application provided at no cost and intended for use "as is". 

This page outlines our commitment to privacy and explains that Kumo is designed to function without collecting any user data. By using this application, you acknowledge the terms described in this policy.

## No Information Collection

**By default and out of the box, Kumo does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, analytics, or crash reports to any internal or external servers.**

Unlike the upstream application, this fork has been modified to remove integrated third-party telemetry services, specifically:
* Google Analytics
* Firebase Crashlytics

All application data, including your library, settings, and reading progress, is stored exclusively and locally on your device. We do not have access to, nor do we share, any of your data with anyone.

## Network Connections and Extensions (User Responsibility)

Kumo acts as a neutral gateway; the application is provided as a "blank" tool without any built-in content or sources. Data transmission only occurs in the following user-initiated scenarios:

* **User Responsibility:** The user is solely responsible for adding extension repositories, installing extensions, and the use they make of them. Kumo does not include, host, or promote any specific extension or repository.
* **Extensions and Content:** When you add a repository and install an extension, any data exchange occurs directly between your device and the third-party source defined by you. Kumo does not monitor or control these connections.
* **Trackers:** If you voluntarily choose to link a tracking service (e.g. AniList, MyAnimeList), your data will be shared with that specific platform according to their own privacy policy to keep your profile updated.

Kumo has no control over, and assumes no responsibility for, the content, legality, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sources, extensions, or services added by the user.

## External Links

The application may contain links to other sites (such as GitHub or documentation). If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that site. Note that these external sites are not operated by us, and we strongly advise you to review their respective Privacy Policies.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect future changes in the application. Any updates will be committed directly to this repository, and you are advised to review this page periodically.

## Contact

If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to reach out by opening an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/nubesurrealista/Kumo).
