<p>An open-source manga reader based on Mihon, <strong>forging its own path</strong>. This app focuses on <b><strong>progressive improvements</strong></b> to enhance the experience, incorporating features from other forks when useful. It offers <b><strong>new themes</strong></b> and promotes a <b><strong>collaborative culture</strong></b>: your theme or feature contributions are welcome! Features: Dracula & Obsidian themes (Kumo exclusives), <b><strong>Trust extensions by default</strong></b> (thanks Kahon), Storage Overview (thanks Aniyomi), persistent search tab, detailed history (thanks Komikku), <b><strong>no Google dependencies</strong></b> - just the essentials for reading, with more to come.</p>
